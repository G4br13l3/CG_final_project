Final Project
Interior design works by Ettore Sottsass

The theme of final project concerns the geometric modeling of some appealing and representative examples of Italian design of the XX century. The choice fell on Italian Architect Ettore Sottsass.

Thus in these pages four home objects designed by Ettore Sottsass are shown. Those are the Casablanca wardrobe, the Carlton library, the Bay table lamp and the Biedermeier sofa.

Prototypes of these objects have been developed by using PLaSM module in Javascript language.
